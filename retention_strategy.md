### Macro Budget Allocation Constraints & Priority Justification
* **Total Global Program Cost Requirements:** **₹215,690.00**
* **CRITICAL CAMPAIGN PRIORITIZATION RUN:** Because our budget envelope is capped, marketing capital must be directed exclusively toward the **At-Risk Spenders** tier first. 
  * *Justification:* This group yields the most immediate ROI protection. They represent large historical capital inflows (Avg Spend: ₹5,382.54) who are actively detaching due to operational friction (Avg Support Tickets: 1.6). Preventing high-value attrition saves far more revenue than trying to reactivate completely cold, low-value dormant users.
